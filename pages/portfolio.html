Video Creation Experience

The Nethender PK Project

With what started as a side project and snowballed into something much greater, Nethender PK is a channel where I show the world what is possible in the space of wheeled sports and parkour, expanding upon what ideas already exist and inventing unique techniques I personally developed.

Here I experiment with the combination of different disciplines that primarily involve merging scooter tricks with Heely tricks, which is a wheeled shoe company that has previously sponsored me. I also work on improving and developing unique ways to use the camera to show the most action I can while making smooth and seamless edits to the music in the video. This has lead to an overall improvement to my simple yet effective editing style.

Sound is just as important in these videos as the video itself, so I always take care to make sure the audience can hear the sound of wheels on pavement as much as they can the music. This substantially adds to the satisfactory of viewing these videos.
