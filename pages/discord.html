Discord Community

We have a community discord server with tons of likeminded (and plenty of non-likeminded) people that make music or just enjoy content from Nethender!

What We Do Here

  • Chill and talk to each other
  • Participate in weekly music challenges
  • Keep up to date with content from Nethender
  • Access to push notifications when any new content comes out

There’s a little bit of something for everyone here, especially if you enjoy breathing air and doing other basic body functions! I mean, I guess if you DON’T breathe, you wouldn’t really be into this server. But if you do breathe, then I’m sure you’ll love it.
